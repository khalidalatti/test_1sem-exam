Adobe illustrator assignment

In the programme Adobe Illustrator, we received a quite different assignment that was targeted the Fabrication Laboratory on UCL. The assignment was to create a logo/image or anything that could be impregnated on a piece of wood. I chose the well known Fendi Logo, that i made into a pattering on the programmed, which then was adjusted to the size of a keychain with my initials in the middle and hole on the top, so it could be linked to my other keys.

Adobe In Design

We had a few assignments in Adobe In Design, where we could create anything that crossed our minds. I thought of making a magazine frontpage, the pictures are from some vacations i was on earlier this year. I’ve illustrated the pictures through Adobe Capture and made them as png’s with different background colours. Prototyping

In this workshop we had to work in groups of 4. Where each group had to create a dating app, with the programme Adobe XD and thereafter make a prototype that showed the concepts idea. Each group had a specific targed group which the app had to be built up after. My group got 80+, which means elderly people. I came up with the name “Haps” and showed the group my version of how the app should’ve looked in Adobe XD. We went with my idea and personally I think it was a great workshop and very different from the other.
